PressureLight (压感光护) is your intelligent safety wearable built for real-world emergencies. This page introduces what PressureLight is, who it's designed for, and how its core features work together to keep you safer.

## What Is PressureLight?

PressureLight is a smart garment equipped with a fabric-embedded pressure sensor matrix, an ESP32 microcontroller, LED strips, and a vibration motor. When you or a bystander triggers an SOS alert, the garment immediately activates visible and tactile alarms — and guides you to share your precise GPS location with an emergency contact via iPhone Safari, all without installing a single app.

PressureLight is designed for:

- **Solo outdoor adventurers** — hikers, cyclists, and trail runners operating in remote areas
- **Night-shift and lone workers** — individuals working in low-visibility or high-risk environments
- **Elderly or mobility-impaired wearers** — anyone who may need to summon help quickly
- **Personal safety-conscious commuters** — people who want a discreet, always-ready alert system

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pressure-Sensing Fabric Matrix" icon="hand-pointer">
    Sensors woven directly into the garment fabric detect sustained pressure and instantly signal the ESP32 to trigger the SOS sequence.
  </Card>
  <Card title="ESP32-Powered LED Alarm" icon="bolt">
    Onboard LED strips flash alternating red and white light, creating a high-visibility alarm that alerts bystanders even in low-light conditions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vibration Motor Alert" icon="bell">
    A built-in vibration motor delivers a strong tactile alert so you know the SOS sequence has activated, even when you cannot see the lights.
  </Card>
  <Card title="High-Accuracy iPhone GPS" icon="location-dot">
    The mobile SOS helper page uses the iPhone's native GPS API in Safari to capture your latitude, longitude, and accuracy in meters with a single tap.
  </Card>
  <Card title="One-Tap Copy or SMS Deep-Link" icon="comment-sms">
    Copy the formatted SOS message to your clipboard or open a pre-filled SMS deep-link that routes directly to your emergency contact — no manual typing required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dual Map Links in Every Message" icon="map">
    Every SOS message includes both an Apple Maps link and a Google Maps link, so your emergency contact can navigate to your location regardless of their device.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No App Install Required" icon="mobile-screen">
    The entire SOS workflow runs inside iPhone Safari as a mobile web page. Your emergency contact needs nothing extra to receive and act on your alert.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Privacy-Safe by Design" icon="shield-halved">
    Your location is never captured silently. GPS coordinates are only read when you explicitly tap 'Get Location', and the SMS is always sent with your manual confirmation.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  PressureLight's mobile SOS helper page is optimized for **iPhone Safari**. For the most reliable GPS accuracy and SMS deep-link behavior, always open the helper page in Safari on iOS. Other browsers may have limited access to the device's location API.
</Note>
