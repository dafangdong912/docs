The PressureLight SOS helper page is your command center when the garment triggers an alert. It runs entirely in iPhone Safari — no app download, no account, no server connection. Spend two minutes familiarising yourself with it now so operating it under stress is second nature.

<Warning>
  Always open the SOS page in **iPhone Safari**. Other browsers such as Chrome or Firefox may not support the `sms:` deep-link format and can provide lower GPS accuracy. The page is designed and tested exclusively for Mobile Safari on iOS.
</Warning>

## Open the Page

You have two options:

- **Scan the QR code** printed on the tag inside your PressureLight garment. Your iPhone camera opens the link directly in Safari.
- **Type the URL** printed on the same tag directly into the Safari address bar.

Both methods take you to the same helper page. Verify you see the blinking red status indicator before continuing.

## Add the Page to Your Home Screen

Saving the SOS page as a Home Screen shortcut lets you open it instantly — the same way you would launch any app.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the page in Safari">
    Navigate to the SOS helper page URL using the QR code or by typing the address into Safari.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the Share button">
    Tap the **Share** button at the bottom of the Safari toolbar — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing upward.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select 'Add to Home Screen'">
    Scroll down the share sheet and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name and confirm">
    Enter the name **PressureLight SOS** so you can find it instantly, then tap **Add**. The icon appears on your Home Screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Page Anatomy

Each section of the helper page has a specific role. Learn them before you need them.

| Element | Chinese Label | What It Does |
|---|---|---|
| **Status indicator** | *(blinking red dot)* | Shows the page is active and ready. A steady or absent dot may indicate a script error — reload the page. |
| **Emergency contact number field** | 紧急联系人手机号 | Accepts the mobile number of your emergency contact. Pre-fills the `sms:` deep-link recipient. |
| **Wearer name field** | 穿戴者姓名 | Your name as it appears in the SOS message. Defaults to **PressureLight 用户** if left blank. |
| **Get Location button** | 获取我的当前位置 | Calls `navigator.geolocation.getCurrentPosition` with high-accuracy mode. Tap this first after opening the page. Location is only captured when you tap — never automatically. |
| **Coordinates display** | *(read-only)* | Shows your latitude and longitude (6 decimal places), accuracy in metres, and the timestamp of the last GPS fix. |
| **SOS message area** | *(read-only textarea)* | The full pre-formatted message: your name, coordinates, accuracy, timestamp, an Apple Maps link (`https://maps.apple.com/?ll=<lat>,<lng>&q=PressureLight%20SOS`), and a Google Maps link (`https://maps.google.com/?q=<lat>,<lng>`). |
| **Copy button** | 复制求助信息 | Copies the complete SOS message to your clipboard via `navigator.clipboard.writeText` (with an automatic fallback for older iOS versions) so you can paste it into any app. |
| **SMS button** | 打开短信发送 | Opens the iPhone Messages app via an `sms:` deep-link with the contact number and SOS message pre-filled. You still tap **Send** to dispatch it. |
| **Open Map button** | 打开地图定位 | Opens your captured coordinates in Apple Maps so you or a responder can visually confirm the location. |

<Note>
  The page does not store your data anywhere. Your name, contact number, and GPS coordinates exist only in the current browser session. Closing the tab clears everything — nothing is sent to any server.
</Note>
