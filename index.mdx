PressureLight (压感光护) is a smart safety wearable that automatically detects distress through pressure-sensitive fabric and alerts the people who matter most. When an SOS event triggers, the garment flashes red and white LEDs, activates a vibration motor, and guides you through sending your precise GPS location to emergency contacts — all from your phone browser, no app required.

## Get Started in 4 Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Charge and wear your garment">
    Ensure your PressureLight garment is fully charged and fitted correctly so the pressure sensors make proper contact with your body.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add an emergency contact">
    Open the SOS helper page and enter your emergency contact's phone number so it's ready when you need it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allow location access">
    When prompted, grant location permission in iPhone Safari so the SOS page can capture your GPS coordinates instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run a test drill">
    Trigger a test SOS to verify lights, vibration, and location sharing all work as expected before heading out.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  PressureLight's SOS helper page works best in **iPhone Safari**. Always open the page in Safari to ensure GPS and SMS deep-links function correctly.
</Note>
